Name:                Miratha de Glacie
ID:                  1
Importance:          1
POV:                 True
Motivation:          She is Harriet's maid from her birth. Originally an illegitamate daughter between a cook and a baron's second son. She herself got pregnant at 15 while working for a merchant who she fell in love with. When she had a miscarriage he tossed her aside as useless to his needs.  Harriet's grandmother on her mother's side found out about this and took her in as a wet nurse to Harriet because Harriet's mother was sickly. Miratha eventually became Harriet's maid, and her close confidante.
                     
                     Has no battle training initially, but after falling in love with the Bandit King, he starts teaching her some basics of defense.
Goal:                Her goal is protect Harriet at all costs.  She sees Harriet as a manifestation of the baby she lost. She would defend her with her everything.
                     
                     Later after falling in love with the Bandit King, she will decide to marry him once she finds a replacement maid for Harriet.
Conflict:            Her love for Harriet and her love for her Bandit King will cause her to choose between them. Harriet orders her to live a happy life. Harriet makes sure the Bandit King chooses Miratha if the need arises.
