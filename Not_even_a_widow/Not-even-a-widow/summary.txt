Situation:   An ugly duckling has to go rescue her betrothed
Sentence:    Harriet is so unwanted that she has to pay her betrothed's ransom when he gets captured during a minor border skirmish with a neighboring nation.
Paragraph:   Harriet, an unremarkable scholar but excellent swordswoman, is taken out of the Noble Academy by her parents when word comes that her betrothed was captured in batlte against a neighboring kingdom. His family does not want to pay the ransom because he is far from the line of succession, and they are currently tight on cash. Her father refuses to help because their fief is also in financial trouble. Harriet decides to take her dowry and her maid to try and ransom him herself. She feels like she has no choice becasuse she isn't very pretty. During her travels she meets a bandit king, charms the nobles of the nearby kingdom, with the king giving her a family heirloom sword. Upon her return, her home kingdom is intriguied by her, leading to some more issues with the royals and the 3rd prince's betrothed lady who becomes jealous.
